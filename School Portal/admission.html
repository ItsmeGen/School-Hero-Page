Admission

Enrolling in PHINMA UPang gives you the privilege of having excellent education in a vibrant community that is nearer to home. PHINMA UPang offers more than 27 undergraduate courses and programs taught in innovative methods to prepare graduates to excel in the competitive professional world.

Official Enrollment Process

    Requirements

  1. PSA Birth Certificate
  2. PSA Birth Certificate
  3. Form 137 (may follow)
  4. For Hawak Kamay (HK) Scholars (if awarded):

    5. HK Certificate

    Step 1

    Marketing Department

    1. Fill up Student Permanent Record (SPR) & Info Sheet
    2. Verification of Required Documents (if available): PSA Birth Certificate High School/Senior High School Report Card (F138) Form 137 (may follow)
    Step 2

    Registrar and Finance

    1. Encoding of SPR
    2. Admission
    3. Submission of Requirement (if available) PSA Birth Certificate High School/Senior High School Report Card (F138) Form 137 (may follow)
    4. Downpayment College Non-scholars: can pay P1,000.00 for registration fee College Scholars: can pay P750.00 for registration fee SHS: can pay P500.00 for registration fee
    5. Issuance of Official Receipt (OR) of down payment.
    Step 3

    Marketing Department

    1. Verification of issued OR number
    2. Printing of ORF (Official Registration Form) or COM (Certificate of Matriculation)
    3. Join designated Facebook groups and fill up ID Application Form
