Scholarship

Most importantly, our education is affordable. We do not charge any hidden or unnecessary fees. We support deserving students throughout their tertiary learning years by offering scholarship programs. Our Hawak Kamay Scholarship Program provides 50% to 75% discount on tuition & miscellaneous fees for our more financially-challenged students.

Check our complete list of scholarships below:
